Chapter 15. FOOD SERVICE ESTABLISHMENTS

§ 17-1410. Exemptions.

§ 17-1410 Exemptions. This chapter shall not apply to antique or hand-made rugs or carpets made of natural fibers such as wool, cotton or jute with no VOC containing carpet cushion or carpet backing.
