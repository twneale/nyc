§ 27-390. Applicability.

* § 27-390 Applicability. This article is applicable to all buildings and existing buildings which have at least one elevator which is subject to periodic inspections pursuant to section 27-998, and any existing office building occupied or arranged to be occupied for an occupant load of more than one hundred persons above or below the street level or more than a total of five hundred persons in the entire building. * NB Amended L.L. 96/85 § 3, language juxtaposed per Ch. 907/85 § 14
