§ 13-398. Annual reports.

§ 13-398 Annual reports. The board shall publish annually in the City Record a report for the preceding year showing the assets of the variable supplements fund and a statement as to the accumulated cash and securities of such fund as certified by the comptroller, and shall set forth in such report such other facts, recommendations and data as the board may deem pertinent.
