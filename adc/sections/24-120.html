§ 24-120. Installation and alteration; permit required.

§ 24-120 Installation and alteration; permit required. No person shall cause or permit the installation or alteration of equipment or apparatus, except as provided in section 24-121 of this code, without first obtaining a permit from the commissioner, and such other licenses or permits as may be required by other governmental agencies and departments.
