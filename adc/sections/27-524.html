Article 2. BASIC REQUIREMENTS

§ 27-524. Tents and air supported structures.

§ 27-524 Tents and air supported structures. Places of assembly enclosed by tents or air supported structures shall comply with the provisions of this subchapter regulating indoor places of assembly, and with the provisions of article nineteen of subchapter seven of this chapter.
