§ 20-466. Prohibitions.

§ 20-466 Prohibitions. It shall be unlawful for any person to sell, give or otherwise transfer any goods or merchandise to an unlicensed general vendor for resale in a public space or to rent or otherwise knowingly transfer any vehicle, stand or pushcart for use by an unlicensed general vendor in a vending business in a public space.
