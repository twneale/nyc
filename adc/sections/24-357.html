§ 24-357. Report to comptroller of expenses and liabilities.

§ 24-357 Report to comptroller of expenses and liabilities. The commissioner of environmental protection shall in every calendar month file in the office of the comptroller an account of any expenses made by the commissioner, or under the commissioner's authority, and of all liabilities incurred by the commissioner during the preceding month and an abstract of each such account shall be published in the City Record.
