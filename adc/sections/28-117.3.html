§ 28-117.3. Duration of certificate.

§28-117.3 Duration of certificate. A place of assembly certificate of operation shall be issued by the department and shall be effective for one year after its issuance. Thereafter, such certificate shall be effective only for periods of time during which there is in effect an annual place of assembly permit issued by the fire department pursuant to section 105.6 of the New York city fire code.
