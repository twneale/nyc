§ 27-880. Equipment not required to be vented.

§ 27-879. General.

§ 27-879 General. (a) Vent systems of gas-fired equipment shall be so designed and constructed as to completely exhaust the products of combustion to the outdoor air. (b) When required by the commissioner, gas vents shall be tested to determine fire safety and the adequate exhausting of the products of combustion. (c) Vents shall be installed if required, in accordance with the applicable standards for gas-fired equipment. See reference standard RS 15-2 for a tabulation of these standards.
