§ 27-882. Chimneys for venting gas-fired equipment.

§ 27-882 Chimneys for venting gas-fired equipment. When venting of gas-fired equipment is provided for by the use of chimneys, such chimneys shall be constructed in accordance with the requirements of this subchapter.
