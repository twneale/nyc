§ 5-302. Construction.

§ 5-302 Construction. The provisions of this subchapter shall apply to all capital project proceeding instituted within the city unless the context or subject-matter otherwise requires, but shall not apply to street closing proceedings, except as provided in section 5-459 of the code, nor to proceedings to acquire real property for rapid transit purposes.
