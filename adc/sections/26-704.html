§ 26-704. Report on status of reserve fund.

§ 26-704 Report on status of reserve fund. The cooperative corporation or condominium board of managers shall report to shareholders and unit owners on a semi-annual basis with respect to all deposits into and withdrawals from the reserve fund mandated by section 26-703 of this chapter.
