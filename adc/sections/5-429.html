Subchapter 4. STREET CLOSING CONDEMNATION PROCEDURE

§ 5-429. Former board of water supply.

§ 5-429 Former board of water supply. Any reference to the former board of water supply occurring in any law, code, contract or document shall be deemed to refer to and mean the commissioner of environmental protection.
