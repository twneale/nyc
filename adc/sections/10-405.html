§ 10-405. Burden of Proof.

§ 10-405 Burden of Proof. Conviction of a crime arising out of the same transaction, occurrence or event giving rise to a cause of action under this chapter shall be considered conclusive proof of the underlying facts of that crime for purposes of an action brought under this chapter. That such crime was a crime of violence must be proved by preponderance of the evidence.
