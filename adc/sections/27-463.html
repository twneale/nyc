§ 27-463. Exterior walls.

§ 27-463 Exterior walls. An exterior enclosure wall shall be required on any side of an open parking structure located within fifteen feet of an interior lot line. Such walls shall be noncombustible construction having at least a two hour fire-resistance rating.
