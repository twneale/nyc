§ 7-509. Storage of property; payment.

§ 7-509 Storage of property; payment. The sheriff is authorized to store any goods or property for the safe keeping of which the sheriff may at any time be responsible, or to designate proper and competent persons to act as keepers or custodians of such goods or property, and to fix the salary of such keepers subject to review by a justice of the supreme court.
