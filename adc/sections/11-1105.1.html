§ 11-1105.1. Credit for rebates of charges for energy.

§ 11-1105.1 Credit for rebates of charges for energy. A taxpayer shall be allowed a credit against the amount of taxes imposed by this chapter for the amount of special rebates and discounts made in accordance with the provisions of section 22-602 of this chapter and for the amount of special rebates and discounts made in accordance with the provisions of section twenty-five-bb of the general city law. Such credit shall be applied against the amount of tax otherwise required to be paid as provided in subdivision a of section 11-1105 of this chapter and shall be claimed for the taxable period immediately succeeding the taxable period in which such rebates or discounts are made.
