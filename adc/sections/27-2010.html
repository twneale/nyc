§ 27-2010. Cleaning of roofs, yards, courts and other open spaces.

§ 27-2010 Cleaning of roofs, yards, courts and other open spaces. The owner of a dwelling containing two or more dwelling units, and the occupant of a single family dwelling shall keep the roof, yard, courts and other open spaces clean and free from dirt, filth, garbage or other offensive material.
