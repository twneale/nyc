§ 16-430. Application by the department of collected covered electronic equipment toward recycling goals.

§ 16-430 Application by the department of collected covered electronic equipment toward recycling goals. The department shall be allowed to apply the amount of covered electronic equipment and orphan waste collected by manufacturers pursuant to this chapter towards achieving its recycling goals.
