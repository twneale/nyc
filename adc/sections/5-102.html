§ 5-102. Additional items in report of comptroller.

§ 5-102 Additional items in report of comptroller. On or before the first day of September in each year the comptroller shall submit to the mayor, the board of estimate and the council a statement showing the funded debt of the city at the close of business on the preceding thirtieth day of June and a statement setting forth the constitutional debt-incurring power, or debt limit, of the city as of the preceding first day of July.
