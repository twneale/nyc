§ 22-267. Investigations by the department of investigation.

§ 22-267 Investigations by the department of investigation. The commissioner of the department of investigation may conduct a study or investigation of any matter arising under the provisions of this chapter, including, but not limited to, investigation of the information required to be submitted by applicants for registration or photo identification cards.
