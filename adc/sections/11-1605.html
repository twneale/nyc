§ 11-1605. Presumptions and burden of proof.

§ 11-1605 Presumptions and burden of proof. For the purpose of proper administration of this chapter and to prevent evasion of the tax hereby imposed, it shall be presumed that all sales of plastic containers are taxable, and not entitled to any credit allowed against the taxes imposed hereby. Such presumptions shall prevail until the contrary is established and the burden of proving the contrary shall be upon the taxpayer.
