§ 28-217.1. Definition.

* § 28-217.1 Definition. For purposes of this article, the terms "Safety Compliance Officer" and "SCO" shall mean a person whose presence is required by the commissioner pursuant to section 28-217.2 and who satisfies the qualifications of section 28-217.4. * NB There are 2 § 28-217.1's
