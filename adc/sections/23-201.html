Chapter 3. NON-EMERGENCY CITY SERVICES

§ 23-201. Municipal reference library, to have official reports, et cetera.

§ 23-201 Municipal reference library, to have official reports, et cetera. The head of each agency shall transmit to the municipal reference library, four copies of each annual or quarterly report or other publication of such agency immediately after the same shall have been issued.
