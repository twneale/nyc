§ 27-938. Size of standpipe risers.

§ 27-938 Size of standpipe risers. The size of standpipe risers shall be in accordance with Table 17-1. Table 17-1 Size of Standpipe Risers ======================================================================== Minimum Riser Size Required Height(1) (I.P.S.) ------------------------------------------------------------------------ 150 ft. or less........................ 4 in. Greater than 150 ft.................... 6 in. ======================================================================== Note: (1) The height shall be that of the individual riser to the highest hose outlet (not including manifold outlets) from the level of the entrance floor at street level at which the riser begins.
