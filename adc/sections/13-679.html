§ 13-679. Limitations on obligations.

§ 13-679 Limitations on obligations. The supplemental retirement allowances provided pursuant to this article shall not constitute membership in a pension or retirement system, nor shall the granting of such allowances create a contractual relationship between the city and any fire retired employee. The payments authorized under this article shall not be retroactive.
