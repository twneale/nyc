§ 16-506. Term and fee for license or registration.

§ 16-506 Term and fee for license or registration. a. A license or registration issued pursuant to this chapter shall be valid for a period of two years. b. The commission shall promulgate rules establishing the fee for any license or registration required by this chapter. Such rules may provide for a fee to be charged for each vehicle in excess of one that will transport waste pursuant to such license and for each such vehicle operated pursuant to such registration.
