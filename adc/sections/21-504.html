Chapter 5*. DEPARTMENT OF EMPLOYMENT TRANSITIONAL JOBS PROGRAM

§ 21-504. Supportive Services.

* § 21-504. Supportive Services. Each sponsoring agency must provide such work-related material, safety equipment, clothing (including uniforms, footwear, outerwear and inclement weather gear), tools and other equipment necessary in order for a participant to perform his or her assignment in a safe, appropriate and efficient manner. Failure of the sponsoring agency to comply with this provision shall be grievable under this chapter. * NB The validity of local law 13 of 2000 is currently a subject of disagreement between the Mayor and the City Council. This certification is not intended as a legal opinion as to the validity of the local law, other than certifying the truth of the facts presented herein.
