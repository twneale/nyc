§ 16-426. Disposal ban.

§ 16-426 Disposal ban. a. Beginning July first, two thousand ten, no person shall dispose of covered electronic equipment as solid waste in the city. b. Beginning July first, two thousand nine, no manufacturer shall dispose of covered electronic equipment as solid waste in the city.
