§ 20-460. Hearings.

§ 20-460 Hearings. Unless otherwise specifically provided, notice and hearings upon denial of an application, the suspension or revocation of a license or the imposition of penalties provided in subdivision b of section 20-472 shall be in accordance with the provisions of chapter one of this title and the rules and regulations applicable thereto.
