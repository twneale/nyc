§ 6-207. Release of certain railroad obligations.

§ 6-207 Release of certain railroad obligations. The board of estimate shall be without power to compromise or release any liability or obligation to the city which may be compromised or released pursuant to section one hundred seventy-three, railroad law, but such liabilities and obligations shall be and remain inviolable.
