§ 5-352. Construction.

§ 5-352 Construction. a. The provisions of this subchapter shall apply to the acquisition by the city of real estate outside the city for the purposes of water supply. b. The provisions of section 5-329 of the code shall be construed to apply to this subchapter. c. Nothing in this subchapter contained shall be deemed to repeal the provisions of chapter nine hundred forty-two of the laws of eighteen hundred ninety-six except where said chapter may be inconsistent with the provisions of the eminent domain procedure law.
