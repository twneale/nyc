Section 20-695 
    
 
    §  20-695  Penalty. Any person who shall violate any of the provisions
  of this subchapter shall be subject to a civil penalty of not less  than
  one  hundred  dollars  nor  more  than  five  hundred  dollars  for each
  violation.
