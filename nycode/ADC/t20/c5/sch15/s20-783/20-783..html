Section 20-783. 
    
 
    §  20-783.  Penalties.  Any  person  who  shall  violate  any  of  the
  provisions of this subchapter shall be subject to a civil penalty of not
  less than two hundred fifty dollars nor more than one  thousand  dollars
  for each violation.
