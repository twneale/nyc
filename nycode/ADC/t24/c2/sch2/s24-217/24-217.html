Section 24-217 
    
 
    §  24-217  Exemptions.  The provisions of this code shall not apply to
  the operation or use  of  any  organ,  bell,  chimes  or  other  similar
  instrument  from  on  or  within  any church, synagogue, mosque or other
  house of worship.
