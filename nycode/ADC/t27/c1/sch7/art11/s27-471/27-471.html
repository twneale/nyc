Section 27-471 
    
 
    §  27-471  Elevators.  Passenger  elevators in open parking structures
  shall comply with  the  requirements  of  subchapter  eighteen  of  this
  chapter,  except  that  hoistways  may  be  enclosed with noncombustible
  construction.
