Section 27-412 
    
 
    §  27-412  Sprinklers.  Sprinkler  protection shall be provided in all
  spraying,  dipping,  or  drying  spaces  using  flammable  materials  in
  accordance with the construction requirements of subchapter seventeen of
  this chapter.
