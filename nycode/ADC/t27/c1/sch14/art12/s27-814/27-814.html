Section 27-814 
    
 
    §  27-814 Supports. All suspended type direct-fired unit heaters shall
  be  adequately  supported  by  metal   hangers,   brackets,   or   other
  noncombustible  supports  with  the  clearances  required  in  reference
  standard RS 14-15.
