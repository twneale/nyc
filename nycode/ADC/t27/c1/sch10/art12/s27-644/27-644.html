Section 27-644 
    
 
    §  27-644  Support  for  glass  panels.  Glass shall be firmly held in
  place.   The supports shall  be  of  adequate  strength  to  resist  the
  applicable  design  wind  loads as prescribed in subchapter nine of this
  chapter.
