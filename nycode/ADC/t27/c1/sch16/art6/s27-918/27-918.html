Section 27-918 
    
 
    §  27-918  Existing  gas  meter  rooms. Existing gas meter rooms shall
  comply with the  provisions  of  section  P-115.5(c)  of  the  reference
  standard   RS-16   no   later  than  December  first,  nineteen  hundred
  seventy-two.
