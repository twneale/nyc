Section 28-419.5 
    
 
    §28-419.5  Combined  hearings.  The  department may choose to have the
  underlying violation adjudicated before  the  office  of  administrative
  trials  and  hearings  in  accordance  with  sections 28-419.5.1 through
  28-419.5.3.
